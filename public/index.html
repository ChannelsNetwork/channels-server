Content

As a consumer you will come here to find engaging information and entertainment -- videos, photos, stories, games, and more. Anyone will be able to publish content here and set their own price on it.

Advertisers can enter an auction to determine the price they must pay consumers who see their content.

Channels makes sure that consumers see the fewest and best ads that pay enough to cover the costs of the content they choose to buy.

ChannelCoin

Unlike Google and Facebook, the two largest ad networks, Channels does not participate as a middleman in these transactions.

Every ad a consumer sees is backed by a tiny transaction directly between the advertiser and the consumer. Every post a consumer opens results in a small transaction between the consumer and the one who posted that content.

These transactions are frictionless and anonymous. And all of this is possible because we use a new cryptocurrency called ChannelCoin. Advertisers will buy these coins. Content producers will sell these coins.

The market will determine the fair market value of the content and advertising space based on the price of this currency.

Open and Transparent

Channels is an open-source network. Unlike the big players today who shroud their systems in secrecy, we think consumers, advertisers, and content producers value transparency -- understanding where the money is coming from, where it is going to, and why.

Users in this network remain anonymous, but auctions and transactions are all transparent.
